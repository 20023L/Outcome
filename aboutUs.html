Welcome to the About Us Page.



We are a delivery company that delivers packages throughout New Zealand.

We prioritise delivery time and a low impact to the environment.

Because of this, we have been improving and investing in technology to better meet our goals.

One method that we are implementing is the use of electric/hybrid vehicles for low distance deliveries.

This can greatly decrease the number of greenhouse emmissions while not compromising on delivery time.
